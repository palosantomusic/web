About

PALO SANTO is a Colombian Caribbean coast music band based in Berlin. They capture the vibrant musical essence of the region, blending traditional instruments — alegre, tambora, maracas, gaita, and vocals — with electric bass and piano.

PALO SANTO brings the energy of Barranquilla's Carnival, the heat of Vallenato, and the deep roots of Cumbia, Puya, Mapalé, Bullerengue, and Afro-Colombian rhythms to the stages of Berlin and beyond.
